About Expense Tracker

Welcome to the Expense Tracker App! This app helps you manage your finances by tracking your income and expenses efficiently. With a user-friendly interface, you can gain full control over your budget and make smarter financial decisions.

Features

  • Track daily expenses
  • Set and manage your budget
  • View graphical representation of your spending
  • Secure and easy-to-use interface
  • Get notifications when you approach your expense limit

Easy Budget Management

Easily create and manage budgets for various categories like groceries, entertainment, and bills.

Smart Notifications

Receive notifications when you are close to your spending limit so you can adjust your expenses in time.

Graphical Reports

Get visual reports of your spending patterns, helping you understand where your money goes each month.
